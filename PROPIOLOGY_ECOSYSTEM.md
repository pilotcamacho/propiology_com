Here is a comprehensive and strategic description for each of your three portals, specifically tailored to give your web developer clear direction on their architecture, purpose, and required functionalities.
By separating these domains, you are using a highly effective digital architecture: .org for marketing and community, .com for the actual software product, and your corporate domain for investor relations and business development.
1. www.propiology.org
The Role: The Content, Education, and Community Hub (Top of the Funnel) The Vibe: Welcoming, educational, philosophical, and open-source.
Primary Purpose: This portal acts as your primary marketing and SEO engine. Its goal is to educate the market on the science of Propiología, attract organic traffic, and build a community. It gives away free value to build trust so visitors eventually migrate to the commercial .com platform.
Target Audience: The general public, students, independent coaches, and psychologists seeking self-knowledge and behavioral change resources.
Instructions & Features for the Developer:
Content Management System (CMS): Needs a robust CMS (like WordPress or Webflow) optimized for heavy blogging, articles, and SEO.
Lead Generation (Lead Magnets): Capability to gate downloadable resources (e.g., personality tests, Story Editing templates, meditation audios) behind email capture forms.
Community Directory: A directory or forum feature where certified Propiología experts (coaches, psychologists) can create profiles and advertise their services to the public.
Book Landing Page: A dedicated, high-converting promotional page for the Propiología book.
2. www.propiology.com
The Role: The Commercial SaaS (Software as a Service) Application (The "Personal OS") The Vibe: Secure, high-tech, action-oriented, and conversion-optimized.
Primary Purpose: This is the actual monetization engine and software platform. It houses your AI-driven tools (like the Care-Multiplier and Cognitive Shield) and converts the interest generated on the .org site into recurring subscription revenue
.
Target Audience: Paying B2C consumers and B2B enterprise clients (HR departments, hospital networks, universities, and coaching businesses).
Instructions & Features for the Developer:
Secure Authentication & User Roles: Requires a secure login portal with distinct role-based access (e.g., End-User, Corporate Admin, Healthcare Provider/Coach).
Payment Gateway & Subscription Management: Integration with robust payment processors (like Stripe or Adyen) to handle monthly subscriptions, B2B licensing fees, and automated billing
.
Dynamic Dashboards: The frontend must display complex data simply. End-users need to see their habit loops and biometric feedback, while B2B clients (coaches/HR) need an aggregated "Command Center" dashboard to monitor the "Readiness Scores" and adherence of their employees or patients.
API & Cloud Infrastructure: Needs a scalable backend (AWS/Python) designed to interact with external APIs (like WhatsApp bots for asynchronous micro-learning and wearable device data integration).
3. www.peopleart.co / www.peopleart.com.au
The Role: The Corporate Headquarters, R&D, and Investor Relations The Vibe: Institutional, scientific, authoritative, and trustworthy.
Primary Purpose: This is the corporate "brain" that owns the Intellectual Property (IP) of Propiología. Its purpose is to attract venture capital, secure non-dilutive government funding (like the Australian Industry Growth Program), and close high-level B2B partnerships
. It proves that there is a serious, scientifically backed company behind the apps.
Target Audience: Venture capitalists, impact investment funds (e.g., Alberts Impact Capital, Giant Leap), government grant assessors, and enterprise-level partners
.
Instructions & Features for the Developer:
Corporate Identity & Trust Signals: A clean, minimalist corporate site. It must clearly state the company's legal status (e.g., an incorporated Australian Pty Ltd), which is a strict requirement for accessing major government grants and accelerator programs
.
Team & Expertise Showcase: A dedicated section highlighting your PhD credentials, cross-disciplinary mastery (biomedical engineering, AI, applied statistics), and the advisory board. Investors invest in the founder's capability as much as the product.
R&D Portfolio (Center of Development and Prototyping): A portfolio section detailing your innovation pipeline, proprietary algorithms (biometric computer vision, AI behavioral models), and hardware prototypes.
Secure Data Room/Contact Portal: A secure contact form and potentially a password-protected "Data Room" where interested investors can download pitch decks, technical whitepapers, and financial models.
